+reinforcement

Force sensing capabilities for assitive walking devices

Working with the Shirley-Ryan Ability Lab in Chicago, my team and I researched and developed a custom prototype that would allow rehab therapists measure the amount of force patients were applying downward onto their walker. Patients who have recently undergone open chest surgery are only able to apply a small amount of force downward onto a walker or else they risk rupturing they're healing sternum. My contribution to the project include all of the mechanical design and analysis as well as development of the electronics, strain gauges, and code.

handle with care

Repositioning wheel chair users

Working with the North Center Adult Care Center in Chicago, we developed a special harness that would allow caretakers to reposition immobile wheelchair users more easily.
